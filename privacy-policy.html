AfriBenki Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 21, 2025

At AfriBenki (operated by AfriBenki Global Limited), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your data when you interact with our AI-powered wealth assistant services. Our services are designed to make micro-savings, investments, bill payments, micro-transactions, and financial literacy accessible through simple WhatsApp or Telegram chats - no apps or complex jargon needed.

This policy applies to:

  • Our website at https://www.afribenki.com and any affiliated sites.
  • Our WhatsApp and Telegram chhannels.
  • All related services, including savings plans, investment recommendations, bill payments, and micro-loans.
  • Online interactions, such as emails or customer support queries.
  • Users in Nigeria and other African countries where we operate (currently focused on Nigeria, with expansion to Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa).

By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDP Act), Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), Money Laundering (Prevention and Prohibition) Act 2022 (MLPPA), and other relevant laws.

1. Types of Personal Data We Collect

We only collect data that's necessary to provide our services, ensure security, and comply with regulations. You can browse our website without sharing personal info, but to use features like onboarding, savings, or investments, we'll need some details.

Information You Provide Directly

When you sign up, chat with our AI bot, or use services, you may share:

  • Identity Details: Full name, date of birth, gender, National Identification Number (NIN), Bank Verification Number (BVN), address, and signature (for verification).
  • Contact Details: Phone number (used for WhatsApp/Telegram access and OTP verification), email address.
  • Financial and Goal Details: Savings or investment goals (e.g., "Save N5,000 weekly for emergencies"), risk tolerance, transaction details (e.g., deposit amounts, bill payments), and banking info (e.g., external bank account for mandates).
  • Other Info: Photos (e.g., for KYC if required), device details (e.g., IP address for security), and location (via GPS if you share it for personalized advice).

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our AI assistant (e.g., chat history, session times) to improve services.
  • Device and Location Data: Device type, operating system, browser info, and IP address to prevent fraud and tailor recommendations.
  • Transaction Data: Details of deposits, withdrawals, investments, or payments processed through partners like VFD Microfinance Bank.

Information from Third Parties

  • We may get data from partners (e.g., VFD for wallet verification) or if you're listed as a next-of-kin.
  • For compliance, we access BVN/NIN details from authorized sources during KYC.

We handle sensitive data (e.g., BVN, financial history) with extra care, only collecting it for essential purposes like account creation or anti-fraud checks. By sharing data, you consent to its use as described here, in line with NDP Act requirements for explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to make our services work smoothly and securely. Here's how:

  • Provide and Personalize Services: Onboard you via WhatsApp, Setup yield-savings account, process deposits/bill payments, and send AI-driven nudges or updates.
  • Customer Support: Respond to queries, resolve issues, and offer financial literacy tips.
  • Compliance and Security: Verify identity via BVN/NIN for tiered wallets, prevent fraud, money laundering, or illegal activities, and enforce our terms.
  • Improvements: Analyze usage to enhance our AI bot, website layout, and features.
  • Marketing and Communications: Send updates on new features or surveys, but only with your consent. You can opt out anytime.
  • Research and Analytics: Aggregate anonymized data to understand trends without identifying you.

We don't use your data for unrelated purposes. All processing aligns with NDP Act 2023 principles: lawful, fair, and transparent.

3. Sharing Your Information

We don't sell your data or share it unnecessarily. Sharing happens only when needed, with safeguards like encryption and contracts requiring privacy compliance. Examples:

  • With Partners: VFD Microfinance Bank for wallets/deposits; payment processors for bills/micro-transactions.
  • For Services: Third-party vendors for OTPs, analytics, or compliance checks (e.g., NDPC-audited tools).
  • Legal Requirements: To regulators (NDPR, CBN), law enforcement, or for anti-money laundering (per MLPPA 2022).
  • In Transactions: If you send/receive funds, basic details (e.g., name, amount) may be shared with recipients or intermediaries.
  • Business Transfers: If AfriBenki is acquired, your data may transfer to the new owner, but under similar protections.

4. Your Rights to Your Data

Under the NDP Act 2023, you have control over your data. Contact us at support@afribenki.com to exercise these:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data we hold (we'll verify your identity first).
  • Correction: Update inaccurate info (e.g., change your address).
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Ask us to delete data if no longer needed, subject to legal retention.
  • Objection: Stop processing for marketing or if it impacts your rights (we'll review, but may continue for legal reasons like fraud prevention).
  • Restriction: Pause processing while we verify accuracy.
  • Portability: Get your data in a transferable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop us using data based on consent, but this may limit services.

We'll respond within 1 month (extendable if complex). No fees unless requests are excessive.

5. Account Monitoring and Security

We monitor accounts to detect suspicious activity, per Money Laundering (Prevention and Prohibition) Act 2022 and Nigerian Data Protection Act 2023. This includes disclosing data to authorities for money laundering or terrorism prevention.

Security Measures

  • Technical Safeguards: Firewalls, encryption (e.g., for chats and transactions), secure servers.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized staff access data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Your Role: Use strong passwords/OTPs; don't share login details. We never ask for them via email - report suspicious requests.
  • Incident Response: If compromised, notify us immediately; we'll block your account and investigate.

If you suspect a breach, email support@afribenki.com. We're not liable for breaches due to your negligence (e.g., sharing OTPs).

6. Employee Obligations and Confidentiality

Our team treats your data confidentially. Employees sign NDAs and undergo training on NDP Act compliance. Violations lead to disciplinary action, up to termination and legal penalties.

We uphold confidentiality strictly, only disclosing under legal mandates.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features (e.g., expanded bill payments) or laws. Changes will be posted on https://www.afribenki.com/policy with a 1-month notice (via email or in-app message). Minor updates (e.g., adding functionality without reducing rights) take effect immediately.

If you disagree, stop using services and close your account. Continued use means acceptance. You're liable for pre-closure activities.

8. Correspondence, Surveys, and Retention

  • Correspondence: We store emails, chats, and support tickets to track our relationship, improve service, and investigate issues. Retained per NDPC rules for audits.
  • Surveys/Questionnaires: Optional; we'll explain data use upfront. Responses help us understand demographics.

We delete data when no longer needed, following retention policies (e.g., transaction records for 7 years per anti-money laundering laws).

9. Compliance with Regulations

We comply with:

  • Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDP Act) and NDPR: Appoint a Data Protection Officer, conduct audits, file reports.
  • MLPPA 2022: Anti-money laundering checks.
  • Other: CBN guidelines for fintech.

All data handling is secure, transparent, and accountable.
