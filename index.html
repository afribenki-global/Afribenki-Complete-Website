FAQ

01

What is Benki

Benki is a smart Banking assistant inside your WhatsApp that connects with your existing Bank or MoMo wallet and allows you to pay bills, send or receive money, save/invest funds, and get financial tips and insights.

02

How does it work?

Start with a simple Hi and see how Benki uses smart AI to understand your money needs, irrespective of text, images, or voice notes or language. Over time, Benki learns from how you use it and gets better at serving you efficiently.

03

Security?

Benki is secured with WhatsApp’s Built-In Privacy Protections with a Passcode for Every Payment by a PIN you create during setup. More so, you can secure your Benki chat in a locked folder and also activate more biometric options for enhanced security

04

Support?

Benki works around the clock, 24/7, and is always available to answer any questions or support requirements you might have. Benki can also escalate any further advanced queries to the Human customer support available.

05

Is Benki licensed?

Benki is a registered technology company in a signed partnership with licensed banking partners in each jurisdiction of operations. All our operations are carried out in accordance with regulatory guidelines. More information is disclosed in our Terms and Conditions
